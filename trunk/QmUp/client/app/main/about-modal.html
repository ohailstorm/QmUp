About QmUp!

QmUP is a collaborative playlist app using the Soundcloud database. To log in you need a Facebook account. When logged in, you can easily create new playlists, adding songs and collaborators to it. To become a collaborator you need to be friends on Facebook and also use QmUP. As a collaborator you can also add songs to the playlist, although only the admin (the creator) can decide wheter or not to allow remote skipping of songs.
